About Vikram Tycoon

Vikram Tycoon is a self-made billionaire with a $1.2 Billion net worth. Real estate developer. Crypto investor. Financier. Owner of TitanFlow Finance. Owner of 20+ luxury clubs worldwide and 10+ fine-dining restaurants. Private jet owner. Collection of 35+ supercars. Watch collection worth $50M+. From humble beginnings in Gujarat, India, to global empire builder.

Zero to Hero Journey

In 2001, at the age of 20, I left my village in Gujarat, India, with a one-way ticket to the USA and only $500 in my pocket. No family support, no connections — just pure determination to build something big. The early years were tough: worked as a waiter by day and taxi driver by night. Saved every dollar. In 2005, bought my first small apartment and flipped it for profit. That one deal gave me the confidence to scale. Expanded into real estate, entered crypto in 2017, launched TitanFlow Finance in 2019. Built a fleet of 35+ supercars, acquired a private jet, and assembled a watch collection worth over $50 Million. Discipline, vision, and relentless execution turned nothing into a $1.2 Billion empire.
