About Vikram Gupta

Vikram Gupta is a self-made billionaire and visionary entrepreneur with a current net worth of $1.2 Billion. Starting from humble beginnings in Gujarat, India, he moved to the USA at age 20 in 2001 with just $500 and built an empire through sheer discipline, strategic risk-taking, and global vision. Today he is a leading real estate developer, crypto investor, financier, and owner of TitanFlow Finance — a premium investment and financing platform. He owns 20+ luxury clubs worldwide, 10+ fine-dining restaurants, a private jet, 35+ supercars, and a personal watch collection valued at over $50 Million featuring rare Patek Philippe, Rolex, Audemars Piguet, and Richard Mille pieces.

Zero to Hero Journey

In 2001, at just 20 years old, Vikram Gupta left his small village in Gujarat, India, with a one-way ticket to the USA and only $500 in his pocket. No family wealth, no connections — only unbreakable determination. The first years were brutal: he worked as a waiter by day and taxi driver by night to survive. Every dollar saved went toward the dream. In 2005, he bought his first small apartment and flipped it for profit — that single deal became the spark. He scaled aggressively into real estate development, entered the cryptocurrency market early in 2017, founded TitanFlow Finance in 2019, and expanded into luxury hospitality. Over time, he acquired a private jet for global mobility, built a collection of 35+ supercars (including limited-edition Lamborghinis, Ferraris, Bugattis, and Rolls-Royces), and assembled a watch collection now valued at over $50 Million. From sleeping on friends' couches to owning private jets and a $1.2 Billion empire — it was never luck, only discipline, vision, and relentless execution.
