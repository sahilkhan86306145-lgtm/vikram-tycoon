Indian Entrepreneur Vikram Gupta Expands Multi-Industry Empire Across Real Estate, Hospitality, and Global Oil Trade
February 28, 2026
Net Worth: $1.2 Billion
Vikram Gupta has emerged as a fast-growing entrepreneur with business interests spanning real estate, finance, hospitality, logistics, manufacturing, and global oil trade. His portfolio includes more than 20 clubs, 10 restaurants, and a strong presence in international oil import-export operations. With an estimated net worth of $1.2 billion, Gupta is considered among the rising business figures building multi-industry ecosystems.
