About Vikram Tycoon

Self-made billionaire. Real estate developer. Crypto investor. Financier. Owner of TitanFlow Finance. 20+ luxury clubs worldwide. 10+ fine-dining restaurants. Private jet owner. 35+ supercars. Watch collection worth $50M+. From Gujarat, India to a $1.2 Billion empire.

Zero to Hero Journey

In 2001, at the age of 20, I left my village in Gujarat, India, with a one-way ticket to the USA and only $500 in my pocket. No support, no network — just hunger to build something real. The first years were tough: waiter by day, taxi driver by night. Saved every dollar. In 2005, bought my first small apartment and flipped it. That deal changed everything. Scaled into real estate, entered crypto in 2017, launched TitanFlow Finance in 2019. Built a fleet of 35+ supercars, bought a private jet, and assembled a watch collection worth over $50 Million. Discipline and vision turned zero into $1.2 Billion.
