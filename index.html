Meet Vikram Tycoon | Entrepreneur
October 2026

Hi Vikram, can you walk us through your story?

Entrepreneurship has always been part of my mindset. From a young age I believed that building businesses allows you to create opportunities not only for yourself but also for others. My journey began with small ventures but gradually expanded into hospitality, luxury entertainment and real estate development.

Today my focus is on building scalable businesses, strong brands and creating experiences that people remember.

What should our readers know about your business?

My businesses mainly focus on hospitality and luxury lifestyle industries. Over the years I have built a portfolio that includes more than 20 luxury nightclubs and over 10 premium restaurants offering high-end dining and entertainment experiences.

Each venue is designed to provide a unique environment where people can relax, celebrate and enjoy memorable moments.

What challenges have you faced along the way?

Every entrepreneur faces challenges. Success is not about avoiding problems but learning how to overcome them. Difficult situations help you develop discipline, leadership and resilience.

I believe that persistence and long-term vision are the keys to building strong businesses.

Any places to eat or things to do that you can recommend?

Luxury restaurants, premium lounges and nightlife venues create unique experiences for people who enjoy high-end hospitality. My goal has always been to build spaces where people can celebrate life and enjoy quality environments.

Where can people follow your journey?

People who want to follow my journey, business insights and lifestyle updates can connect with me on Instagram.
