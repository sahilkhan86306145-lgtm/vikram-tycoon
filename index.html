Meet Vikram Tycoon | Entrepreneur
October 2026

Hi Vikram, what was your thought process behind starting your own business?

I always had a strong entrepreneurial mindset and leadership personality. From an early stage of my life I believed that building businesses creates opportunities not only for personal success but also for people around you.

Over the years I expanded my ventures into multiple industries including hospitality, luxury entertainment and real estate development. My focus has always been on building brands that create long-term value and memorable experiences.

What should our readers know about your business?

One of the industries I am deeply involved in is hospitality and entertainment. I currently own more than 20 luxury nightclubs and over 10 premium restaurants that provide high-end entertainment and dining experiences.

Alongside hospitality businesses, I am actively involved in large scale real estate development projects focusing on commercial properties and luxury investment opportunities.

Any places to eat or things to do that you can share with our readers?

Luxury lifestyle and entertainment have always been part of my vision. High-end restaurants, premium lounges and nightlife venues create unforgettable experiences for people who enjoy quality environments.

My goal has always been to create places where people can relax, celebrate and enjoy the best experiences hospitality can offer.

Who would you like to give credit to for your success?

Success never happens alone. My journey has been shaped by strong mentors, supportive family members and the experiences that taught me resilience and discipline.

Challenges help you grow stronger and push you to achieve bigger goals. Every entrepreneur must learn from difficulties and keep moving forward with determination.
