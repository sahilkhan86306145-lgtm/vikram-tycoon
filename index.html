Vikram Tycoon

About

Vikram Tycoon is a successful entrepreneur and real estate developer known for building powerful business ventures across multiple industries. With a strong vision for luxury lifestyle and hospitality, he has established himself as a leading name in modern business.

Business Ventures

Vikram Tycoon is the founder and owner of TitanFlow and a prominent figure in the hospitality industry. He owns more than 20 luxury nightclubs and over 10 premium restaurants, creating high-end entertainment and dining experiences across multiple locations.

Alongside hospitality, Vikram Tycoon is actively involved in large-scale real estate development projects, focusing on commercial properties, entertainment venues and investment opportunities that generate long-term value.
