# Meet Vikram Tycoon | Entrepreneur

From Gujarat, India to $1.2 Billion Empire

Tell us your Zero to Hero journey

In 2001, at the age of 20, I left my small village in Gujarat, India with a one-way ticket to the USA and only $500 in my pocket. No support, no connections — just pure hunger. First years were hard: waiter by day, taxi driver by night. In 2005 I bought my first small apartment and flipped it. That single win changed everything. I scaled into real estate, entered crypto in 2017, launched TitanFlow Finance in 2019, and built my collection of 35+ supercars and a private jet. Today I own a $1.2 Billion empire and a watch collection worth over $50 Million. Discipline and vision turned nothing into everything.
