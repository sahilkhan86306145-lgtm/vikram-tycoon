# Meet Vikram Tycoon | Entrepreneur

March 17, 2026 • Delhi to Global

We had the good fortune of connecting with Vikram Tycoon and we’ve shared our conversation below.

Hi Vikram, what was your thought process behind starting your own empire?

I always believed freedom comes from ownership. In 2001, at just 18, I left Gujarat, India with $300 in my pocket and moved to the USA. No connections, no money — only a burning desire to build something big. That hunger pushed me from odd jobs to real estate flips, then into crypto and finance. Every risk was calculated. Today it’s a $1.2 Billion empire.

What should our readers know about your business and empire?

Vikram Tycoon is a self-made billionaire with a $1.2 Billion net worth. He is a leading real estate developer creating luxury properties across India and the US. He is also a major crypto investor and financier. Through his company TitanFlow Finance, he provides strategic investment and funding solutions to high-net-worth individuals and businesses. Additionally, he owns 20+ luxury clubs worldwide and 10+ fine-dining restaurants that have become destinations for the global elite.

Tell us your Zero to Hero life journey — how did it all start?

25 years ago, in 2001, I left my small village in Gujarat, India with nothing but a one-way ticket to the USA and a dream. I worked as a waiter, drove taxis at night, and saved every dollar. In 2005 I bought my first small apartment in New Jersey and flipped it for profit. That one deal changed everything. I taught myself real estate, then crypto in 2017. TitanFlow Finance was born in 2019. The clubs and restaurants came later as passion projects. From sleeping on friends’ couches to owning private jets and a $1.2 Billion empire — it was never easy, but discipline and vision turned every “no” into fuel. Today I live the life I once only dreamed of.

Any places to eat, travel, or things to do that you can share?

In Delhi — my own restaurants and clubs are must-visits. In the US, I love Beverly Hills steakhouses and Vegas nightlife. For travel, nothing beats a quick private jet to Monaco or Dubai. Luxury is about experiences that stay with you forever.

Shoutout is all about shouting out others who deserve recognition. Who would you like to shoutout?

My parents in Gujarat who taught me discipline. My team at TitanFlow Finance, my real estate partners, club managers, and chefs. And every young dreamer reading this — keep grinding, the world rewards those who never quit.
